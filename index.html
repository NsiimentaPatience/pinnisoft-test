About Us

What makes us the best choice?

PinniSOFT is established by a team of qualified & experienced professionals to create solutions for startups, entrepreneurs, mid-size businesses, organizations, governments, banks, hotels & food industry, telecommunications, NGOs, and corporate entrepreneurs.

As a leading, highly innovative software firm, systems integrator, and technology provider, PinniSOFT delivers intelligent technical solutions and consulting services that enable efficient, secure access and communication with various heterogeneous information resources and services, anytime and anywhere.

PinniSOFT builds engaging custom systems, software, and apps, while offering consulting services in Data Science & Analytics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Information Technology, Networking, and Cyber Security. We believe in building digital products for people, by people. Our focus is to bring change and innovation into the tech world by creating better products and delivering cutting-edge IT solutions.
